About me


My name is Ioannis Psarros and I am a Postdoctoral researcher at the University of Bonn, working with Prof. Anne Driemel. Before that, I obtained my PhD from the University of Athens, Greece, under the supervision of Prof. Ioannis Z. Emiris.
